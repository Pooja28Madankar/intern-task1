trying katori chat?

about katori chat

an unique chaat recipe or a street food served in crisp snack deep fried bowl’s. the bowl or canopies is filled with potatoes, sprouts finely chopped onions and tomatoes with chaat chutney’s and served as individual snack. it can be an ideal party starter snack and certainly can raise lot of curiosity with your party guests

4. ingredients?

▢1 cup chickpea / chana (boiled) ▢1 potato / aloo (boiled & cubed) ▢¼ cup green chutney ▢1 cup moong sprouts ▢½ cup tamarind chutney ▢1 cup curd / yogurt ▢sprinkle kashmiri red chilli powder ▢sprinkle cumin powder / jeera powder ▢1 onion (finely chopped) ▢1 to mato (finely chopped) ▢½ cup sev ▢few coriander leaves ▢sprinkle chaat masala ▢sprinkle salt
