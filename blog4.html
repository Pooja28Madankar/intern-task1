how hakka noodle is made?

Image result for hakka noodles recipe A hakka noodle is made from unleavened dough( rice or wheat flour) that is cooked in a boiling liquid. Depending upon the type, noodles may be dried or refrigerated before cooking. A typical preparation involves splashing of oil to a large pot of boiling salted water and cooking the noodles as directed on the package.
